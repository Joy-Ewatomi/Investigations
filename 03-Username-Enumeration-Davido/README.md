### What I Learned:

**1. Tool Selection Matters**
Different tools serve different purposes:
- **Investigation = WhatsMyName** (depth, accuracy)
- **Quick Check = Namechk** (speed, simplicity)
- **Best Practice = Use BOTH** (complementary strengths)

**2. Suspended Accounts Have Value**
A suspended account doesn't erase history:
- Past interactions remain visible
- Historical mentions persist
- Connections reveal prior legitimacy
- Timeline analysis shows account evolution

**3. Official Platforms Provide Ground Truth**
Verified music platforms (Amazon, Spotify, Apple Music) establish baseline:
- Professional verification processes
- Less prone to impersonation
- Connect directly to rights holders
- Reliable for artist identity

**4. Verification is Multi-Source**
Never rely on single indicator:
- Check multiple platforms
- Cross-reference accounts
- Look for historical interactions
- Verify through official channels

**5. Context Matters for Follower Counts**
Low followers doesn't automatically mean fake:
- New platforms = fewer users
- Migration from other services
- Not all accounts promoted equally
- Consider platform popularity
